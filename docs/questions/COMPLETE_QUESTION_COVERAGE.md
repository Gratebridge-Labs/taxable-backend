# Complete Question Coverage - Tax Engine

## Overview
This document outlines ALL questions needed for complete tax filing, covering every angle and data point required by FIRS.

## Question Flow Structure

### Phase 1: Base Questions (Qualifying)
**Purpose**: Determine which detailed questions to ask
- Individual: 6 base questions
- Business: 6 base questions
- Joint Spouse: 1 base question
- Joint Business: 2 base questions

### Phase 2: Personal/Business Information
**Purpose**: Collect basic identification and contact information

**Individual:**
- Full name (first, middle, last)
- Date of birth
- Residential address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Occupation
- Marital status

**Business:**
- Business name
- Registered name (if different)
- Business address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Website (optional)
- Commencement date

### Phase 3: NIN/TIN Collection
**Purpose**: Collect tax identification numbers

**Individual:**
- NIN (required, 11 digits)
- TIN (if available, 10-12 digits)

**Business:**
- Business TIN (required)
- CAC Registration Number (if registered)

**Joint Spouse:**
- Spouse 1 NIN and TIN
- Spouse 2 NIN and TIN

**Joint Business:**
- Business TIN
- Each partner's NIN and TIN

### Phase 4: Income Collection (Detailed)

#### Employment Income
- Employer name
- Employer address
- Employer TIN
- Employment start date
- Employment end date (if applicable)
- Annual gross salary
- Basic salary
- Housing allowance
- Transport allowance
- PAYE deducted
- Benefits-in-kind (type, value, description)

#### Business Income
- Total business revenue/turnover
- Revenue by source (sale of goods, services, etc.)
- Other income (dividends, interest, etc.)

#### Rental Income
- Number of properties
- Per property:
  - Property address
  - Annual rental income
  - Repairs & maintenance
  - Management fees
  - Insurance
  - Interest on property loans
  - Other expenses

#### Investment Income
- Income type (dividends, interest, capital gains)
- Amount
- Tax withheld at source
- Source/description

### Phase 5: Deductions Collection (Detailed)

#### NHF Contributions
- Total NHF contribution for tax year

#### NHIS Contributions
- Total NHIS contribution for tax year

#### Pension Contributions
- Total pension contribution for tax year
- Pension fund administrator name

#### Life Insurance
- Total premium paid
- Insurance company name

#### Rent Relief
- Total annual rent paid
- Rented property address
- Rent receipts available (yes/no)

#### Mortgage Interest
- Total interest paid on mortgage
- Lender name
- Property address

### Phase 6: Business Expenses (Detailed)

#### Operating Expenses
- Expense category
- Amount
- Description
- Period

**Categories:**
- Rent and lease
- Utilities
- Salaries and wages
- Office supplies
- Marketing and advertising
- Professional services
- Transportation
- Insurance
- Repairs and maintenance
- Interest on business loans
- Bad debts
- Research and development
- Pension contributions (for employees)
- NHF contributions (for employees)
- Other

#### Research & Development
- Total R&D expenditure (max 5% of turnover)

#### Donations
- Recipient name
- Recipient type
- Amount
- Date

### Phase 7: Capital Assets (Detailed)

#### Individual Business Assets
- Asset type
- Description
- Cost
- Date acquired
- Is new asset

#### Company Assets
- Asset class (Building, Plant, Vehicle, etc.)
- Description
- Cost
- Date acquired
- Is new asset
- Previous capital allowance claimed

**Asset Classes:**
- Building (10% allowance)
- Plant and Machinery (20% allowance)
- Motor Vehicle (25% allowance)
- Furniture and Fittings (20% allowance)
- Software (25% allowance)
- Agricultural Equipment (20% allowance)
- Mining Equipment (20% allowance)
- Other Equipment (20% allowance)

### Phase 8: Capital Gains (Detailed)

#### Asset Disposals
- Asset type
- Description
- Date acquired
- Date disposed
- Acquisition cost
- Disposal proceeds
- Disposal expenses

**Asset Types:**
- Principal private residence (exempt once in lifetime)
- Shares/stocks
- Property (investment)
- Motor vehicle
- Personal chattels (exempt if <₦5M)
- Other

### Phase 9: Employees (Business)

#### Employee Information
- Number of employees
- Total payroll (salaries and wages)
- PAYE deducted and remitted
- Pension contributions for employees
- NHF contributions for employees

### Phase 10: Special Business Categories

#### Small Company Details
- Total fixed assets value
- Professional services? (disqualifies small company status)

#### Agricultural Business
- Agricultural activities
- Business commencement date
- Agricultural income

#### Export Business
- Export revenue
- Proceeds repatriated through official channels?
- Export products/services with amounts and destinations

### Phase 11: Dependents (Individual)

#### Dependent Information
- Name
- Relationship
- Age
- In school?

**Maximum:** 4 dependents

### Phase 12: Exemptions & Special Cases

#### Individual Exemptions
- Minimum wage earner?
- Agricultural business worker (first 5 years)?
- Military officer?
- Combat zone/hazardous area worker?

#### Business Exemptions
- Export processing zone entity?
- Insurance business?
- Lottery/gaming business?
- Collective investment scheme?
- Mining operations?
- Priority sector (economic development incentive)?
